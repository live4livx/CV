HTML Tags You Should Know

  • <html> – Root element of an HTML document.
  • <head> – Contains meta info like title, links, and scripts.
  • <body> – All visible content goes here.
  • <h1>...<h6> – Headings from largest to smallest.
  • <p> – Paragraphs.
  • <a> – Links to other pages.
  • <img> – Images.
  • <div> – Generic container.
  • <span> – Inline container.
  • <ul>, <ol>, <li> – Lists (unordered, ordered, list items).
